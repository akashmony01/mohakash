## The brief

One home for four selves — freelance developer, researcher, poet, and someone
writing in public about building with Claude. The challenge was making those
feel like *one* coherent person rather than four stitched-together sites.

## The approach

The name does the work. *Mohakash* — **moh** for Mohammed, **akash** for Akash —
also means *the infinite sky*. So the whole site is a night sky: deep indigo, a
faint starfield, a sky-blue accent with a rare gold spark, and serif headlines
over clean sans body.

Built static with Astro, styled with Tailwind, and sprinkled with just enough
Alpine.js for the menu, contact form, and blog filter. Hosted on GitHub Pages
behind Cloudflare, with the contact form handled by a small Worker.

## The result

A fast, quiet space that loads instantly and reads like a single considered
voice — room for code, verse, and inquiry to sit side by side.
