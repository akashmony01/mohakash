---
title: "The Infinite Sky"
date: 2026-05-01
note: "The poem the domain is named for."
---
They asked me where I'm from.
I pointed up.

Akash, my mother called me —
sky, the thing that holds
without ever closing its hands.

So I learned to keep my edges open:
room for a stranger's weather,
room for the slow arrival of stars,
room, even, for the dark
between them.
