For years my work lived in scattered places: code in repos, poems in notebooks,
research in half-finished documents, thoughts in apps that own them.

This site is an attempt to gather them under one sky — *mohakash*, which is both
my name (**moh**ammed **akash**) and the word for the infinite sky.

Not a portfolio exactly. More like a window: here's what I'm building, reading,
writing, and wondering about, all at once. If any of it resonates, the contact
form is right there on the home page.
